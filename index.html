About IIT



HISTORY INTRNATIONAL INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY (IIT)


It is a higher private education body approved by the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research. It was inaugurated on August 23, 2012.


Who is an engineer ?


The engineer is a professional capable of analyzing complex problems, designing plans or processes, executing and controlling the completion of tasks according to the objectives and needs requested.


Why i should go with engineering ?


Choosing engineering in your academic career allows you to have access to all professional fields and sectors of activity in Tunisia or around the world.


Engineering with IIT


The International Institute of Technology of Sfax offers its students solid training in engineering in the fields of Civil Engineering , Computer Engineering , Process Engineering , Mechanical Engineering , Telecommunications Engineering and Industrial Engineering .


These training courses are well developed and adapted according to the future needs of the national and international market and aim to prepare our engineers to confront and solve the various problems encountered.


The engineering training program and the study plan for each training offered by the IIT are developed according to a well-defined vision and a very specific objective. The axes are set by experts in the field and educational advisers according to recruitment trends and changes in the design of employability.


Access to the engineering cycle requires training for a bachelor's degree, or two-year training in the preparatory cycle.


The IIT engineering school offers its students high-level engineering training . With the aim of enriching the skills of its students, IIT organizes specific training and events with the collaboration of its national and international partners.
